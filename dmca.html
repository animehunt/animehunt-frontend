DMCA Notice & Takedown Policy

AnimeHunt respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. We comply with the provisions of the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

AnimeHunt does not host any files on its own servers. All media files available on this website are hosted by third-party services over which we have no control. AnimeHunt only provides links to content that is already available on the internet.

If you are a copyright owner or an agent thereof and believe that any content accessible on AnimeHunt infringes your copyrights, you may submit a DMCA takedown request. Upon receiving a valid notice, we will remove the infringing links as soon as possible.

A valid DMCA takedown notice must include the following information:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • The exact URL(s) of the allegedly infringing material.
  • Your full name and contact information.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information provided is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.

Any incomplete or false DMCA notices may be rejected.
