About AnimeHunt

AnimeHunt is an anime streaming and discovery platform created for anime lovers who want a clean, fast, and mobile-friendly experience. Our goal is to make it easy for users to explore anime, movies, cartoons, and series in one place.

We do not host any media files on our servers. All videos and download links available on AnimeHunt are provided by third-party websites. We only index and organize content to help users find anime more easily.

AnimeHunt focuses on performance, simple design, and ease of use. The website is optimized for mobile devices and aims to deliver a smooth browsing experience with minimal loading time.

We respect copyright laws and take content removal requests seriously. If you believe that any content linked on AnimeHunt violates your rights, please contact us through the method provided below.
